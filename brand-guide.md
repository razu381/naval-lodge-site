# NEXCOM Hospitality Group — Brand Guidelines

---

## 1. Introduction

This guide outlines the brand identity of the **Navy Exchange Service Command (NEXCOM) Hospitality Group**.

Its purpose is to:
- Ensure **consistent communication and design**
- Strengthen overall **brand recognition**
- Improve the **effectiveness of messaging**

---

## 2. Brand Overview

The NEXCOM Hospitality Group includes two primary entities:
- **Navy Lodge** (36 locations)
- **Navy Inn** (59 locations)

Both brands:
- Deliver **award-winning hospitality**
- Focus on **5-star guest service**
- Provide **high-quality accommodations at the best value worldwide**

---

## 3. Brand Positioning

The Hospitality Group stands for:
- Reliability and trust
- Comfort and convenience
- Value-driven hospitality
- Service excellence for military personnel and families

---

## 4. Brand Terminology

### Formal Naming Convention
- **Navy Lodge by NEXCOM Hospitality Group**
- **Navy Inn by NEXCOM Hospitality Group**

### Usage Rules
- First mention: Use **full formal name**
- Subsequent mentions: Use **Navy Lodge** or **Navy Inn**
- ❌ Do **not** use acronyms

---

## 5. Brand History & Background

### Navy Lodge
- Established in **1969**
- Designed to serve **Sailors and their families**

**Key Offerings:**
- Oversized rooms & family suites
- Free Wi-Fi
- Complimentary breakfast
- Pet-friendly locations

**Eligibility:**
- Active duty
- Reservists
- Retirees
- Veterans
- DoD personnel
- NEXCOM associates

---

### Navy Inn
- Formerly **Navy Gateway Inn & Suites (NGIS)**
- Full operational control under NEXCOM achieved on **Jan 30, 2022**
- Rebranded to **Navy Inn in 2023**

**Positioning:**
- Preferred lodging for **TDY (Temporary Duty) travelers**
- Focus on **value and convenience**

**Eligibility:**
- Active duty & reservists
- Retirees
- NAF/DoD civilians
- Sponsored guests

---

## 6. Logo Usage

### Primary Logo
- Represents the official identity of the brand
- Must be used in all primary communications

### Alternate Logo
- Used when the primary logo is not suitable due to layout or space constraints
